FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

During the time our app has been up in the air we haven gotten the same questions about 100 times. This is why we have decided to make this frequently asked questions webpage. We hope that this page answers many of your questions and if there are any missing, you can report them to us through the contact form.

Which platforms does the app support?

Our app currently has support for the android platform

Where do I find my own tweets?

Your tweets can be found on your profile, you get there by clicking on your profile image at the top left.

Why can't I tweet?

When you can't tweet, you probably have a problem with your network connection. Please check if your Wi-Fi or cellular network is enabled.
